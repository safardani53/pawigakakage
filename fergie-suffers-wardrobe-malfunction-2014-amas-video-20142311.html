Fergie Suffers Wardrobe Malfunction During 2014 AMA Performance: Video

Stripping off your clothes isn’t always a good thing, Fergie Ferg! At the 2014 American Music Awards on Sunday, Nov. 23, Fergie hit the stage singing her new bounce-worthy single “L.A. Love.” 

After a sweet introduction from hubby Josh Duhamel (“She makes really cute babies!”), the Black Eyed Peas crooner, 39, danced on top of a bus, jamming out to her new tune. Wearing platform sneakers, a one-piece swimsuit, and a robe wrapped around her middle, Fergie performed most of the song without a hitch.
