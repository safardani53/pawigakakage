BREAKFAST Television host Meredith Shaw called out her co-host on stage after he bragged about his outfit.

Sid Seixeiro is the usual co-host alongside Meredith for BT and the pair appeared on stage for their Christmas special of the show on Friday.

The pair opened the segment on stage at the Casa Loma Museum in Toronto, Canada.

Meredith started her first year of Spirit of the Season by dancing and smiling on stage to the opening music.

The special Christmas episode takes place every year and welcomes a live audience to the show for festive celebrations and prize-giving.

Sid and Meredith gushed over the audience and seemed surprised at the huge turnout.

Sid stopped the audience cheering by holding out his arms.

He exclaimed: "Hang on a second. Meredith Shaw everyone, she looks amazing. She looks amazing."

Meredith walked off looking embarrassed and covered her face before twirling her long skirt around.

She joked: "What? This old thing?"

DRESSED TO IMPRESS

Meredith wore a long sheer green dress that contained puff sleeves, pleats and ruffles all over.

It was on top of a high-neck long dress that flattered her body and showed off her curves.

Her hair was sleeked back into a high up-do and she donned full glam makeup.

Sid also sported a festive look and wore a bedazzled gray suit with a silk tie.

The audience laughed and cheered in response to Meredith before falling silent.

BRAGGING?

Sid then looked to the audience and stepped forward proudly with his arms out at his side.

He bragged: "And I look great too."

The audience cheered as he surveyed them seriously.

He seemed to feign embarrassment: "Stop. Stop."

Sid lowered his arms and then put them out again to encourage the audience to cheer again.

Meredith stepped forward and held him by the shoulder so he stepped back to her level.

She gave a sly dig: "But of course Sid, it's not about us.

"It's about the wonderful BT viewers who are here and watching from home this morning live from Casa Loma for Spirit of the Season."

The audience erupted in cheers.

CHRISTMAS SPIRIT

The rest of the show included several festive specials, including gift giving.

Santa Claus even made a special appearance on the show, claiming that Sid had made it onto the "Nice List," this year.

"Let's go," Sid shouted on stage and cheered.

Later weather reporter Frank Ferragine made an appearance to interview Santa Claus.

However, Santa admitted he was on the "Naughty List."

Frank gave a shoutout to Stephanie Henry and Tammie Sutherland who remained in the studio, and Santa admitted they had also made the "Nice List" after Steph visited his "Summer house."
