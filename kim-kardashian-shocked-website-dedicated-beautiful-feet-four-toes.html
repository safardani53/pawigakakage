KIM Kardashian was shocked by a website dedicated to her “beautiful FEET.”

The 40-year-old stumbled across the website after fans accused her of having “only four toes.”

After promoting socks from her SKIMS line, Kim took to her Instagram Story to show her followers a WikiFeet page dedicated to her.

While scrolling through close-up photos of her feet, the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star said: “Oh my god! Look at my profile. Who made this?”

Kim was heard laughing off screen as she continued to scroll through the website, which features a plethora of photos that show off the reality star’s feet.

A shocked Kim continued: “There’s a whole site called WikiFeet that literally shows little piggies."

'BEAUTIFUL FEET'

After she noticed a feature that scores each person’s feet, Kim learned that she has “beautiful feet.”

The KUWTK star happily squealed before she shared her “one trick” about maintaining her “beautiful” feet.

“But in sneakers, I wear a size eight.

“Or in men’s sneakers I wear a size 6 and a half because I like my sneakers really big and I don’t like my toes.”

After her friend joked Kim would wear a size 10 shoe “for fashion,” the mother of four responded: “I’ll wear anything.”

Kim learned about the website after fans accused her of only have “four toes.”

KIM'S 'FOUR TOES'

Back in May, the SKIMS founder was accused of a photoshopping her feet after eagle-eyed fans claimed she only had four toes in a bikini pic.

After Kim shared a snap of herself posing in a tiny bikini on the beach, some fans expressed their concern with her bare foot- which was perched on a tree stump- and began to question if she had "cropped a toe off."

Commenting on the star's post on Twitter, one fan penned: "Is it just me or is there only 4 toes?"

"Kim why it look like u got 4 toes?" another commented.

SIX TOES?

The speculation in May is not the first time Kim’s toes have gotten the attention of fans.

Her toes first came under scrutiny in 2019, when KUWTK fans believed they spotted a bonus toe on her left foot.

She was then accused of having six toes once again in a snap from her controversial birthday trip in October.

KIM ADDRESSES SPECULATION

Last September, Kim was forced to address speculation over her feet on Instagram after fans claimed they spotted an extra toe.

She took to Instagram to "prove" she didn't have a bonus toe, nor suffer from polydactyly, the term given to someone born with extra fingers or toes.

In a series of videos about her feet, Kim said: "Everyone thinks that I have six toes, and it's really wild."

After counting her five toes, the Skims founder explained that what people believed was an extra toe is in fact the side part of her foot.

She continued: "But it's this, part of my foot, that when I wear a shoe just like this it like smashes down right here and in a picture.
