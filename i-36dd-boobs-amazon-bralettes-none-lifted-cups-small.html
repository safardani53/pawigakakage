A BUSTY woman has put trendy bedazzled Amazon bralettes to the test, but was disappointed by each of them.

Shelli, who goes by the TikTok handle @stylenright, is midsize, petite, curvy and busty, so she shares shopping advice and style inspiration for women with similar body types.

In one TikTok video, she did an Amazon shopping haul but found that the pieces couldn’t properly support her breasts and weren’t true to size.

“I ordered some of these trending, blinged-out bralettes to see if they work for us large-chested gals,” she said.

Shelli is a size 36DD in bras and a size 12 in clothes, so she wanted to see if some viral and festive bra tops could compliment her body shape.

First she tried on an irridescent purple and black bedazzled corset-style top. 

“It’s giving me Madonna in the 90s vibes,” she joked.

Shelli was wearing the ELLACCI Rhinestone Bustier Crop Top from Amazon. It’s available in sizes XS to Large and retails between $38 and $43.

“Let’s start with the cups,” she said, adding: “The bottom of my bust is way down here,” as she pointed out that the cups were much smaller and higher than her natural breast shape.

She attempted pulling the top down, which left more of her chest exposed, but ultimately said that her breasts were still too big for the cups.
