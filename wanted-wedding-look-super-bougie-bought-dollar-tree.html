I wanted my wedding to look super bougie so I bought a must-have at Dollar Tree - it was a huge hit

A BRIDE has candidly revealed how she saved on entertaining guests at her wedding.

A simple swap from the Dollar Tree became a hit with guests at the matrimonial event.

TikTok account @theweddingswap shared a video of the bride detailing the product in a video on the social media platform.

"I had Dollar Tree champagne coupes at my wedding," she said.

"I wanted it to look super bougie, and it really, really did.

"I ordered 75 champagne coupes for $75 off dollar tree.

"They were a huge hit and I had a champagne tower," the bride added.

In yet another video she gave further detail on the winning Dollar Tree purchase.

"I went on Dollar Tree [dot] com and got 100 champagne coupes for $100," she said.

"I think it was like $15 for shipping. I got so many compliments and it was like the best decision. Super nice coupes," she added.

A six-count of the champagne glasses retail for $1.25 on the Dollar Tree website.

The videos drew in many viewers who shared their two cents on the affordable wedding buy.

"We did the same thing," one wrote.

"I order lots of glassware from there for entertaining. Plates, wine glasses, coffee cups, champagne flutes. They come packed when ordered," another added.

"What?" a third commented. "I just bought two long-stemmed coupes for $36 on Amazon."

"I love the look of coupes over flutes," yet another wrote.

"I did Dollar Tree, half price Hobby Lobby, and Oriental Trading for mine. Saved any way I could without going tacky," one more wrote.
