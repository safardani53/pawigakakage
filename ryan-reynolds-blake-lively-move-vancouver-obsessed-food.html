Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively 'to move to Vancouver' because actress is 'obsessed' with city's food

RYAN Reynolds and Blake Lively have revealed their plans to move to Vancouver, Canada, due to the actress' love of the food there in a new interview.

The famous couple currently reside in Westchester County, New York, with their four children.

In a sit down with the Vancouver Sun, Ryan - who is a Canada native - shared that they've taken steps to relocate.

"We’ve been — I guess I could characterize it as — subtly looking for a while," the Deadpool actor confessed to the publication.

He then explained that a big part of the reason for the move is Blake's "obsession" with the city's food.

“Thankfully my wife is obsessed with Vancouver and the food scene in Vancouver," Ryan admitted.

The movie star then opened up about his upbringing outside of the United States and how its helped him excel in his career.

"I’m obsessed with the childhood that I was given and how privileged that was to grow up in a place like Vancouver, which I think has imbued tenants that I have taken and carried with me throughout my entire career," the Adam's Project star said.

"…It has really given me the tools to process and deal with a lot of the things maybe some of my peers maybe have slightly more maladaptive coping mechanisms to deal with.”
