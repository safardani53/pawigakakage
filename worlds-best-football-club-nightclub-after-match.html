Inside the world's best football club' that turns into epic nightclub open until 5am after every

A BELGIAN football club has been dubbed the world’s best after a social media user discovered the stadium doubles as a nightclub.

KV Oostende (KVO) has gone viral on TikTok thanks to a user putting footage of the side’s Diaz Arena on the site.

The ground not only hosts club nights that go on until the early hours but also hosts concerts, galas, weddings and parties.

The Belgian side often hosts parties for its fans that start straight after the final whistle has blown on a Saturday and continues until 5am.

The TikTok user known as Robdidatiktok, who posted the footage, said: "I think I've found the best football club in the world.

"And you will not believe what happens after they do when the match is finished.

“This is KV Oostende in Belgium and it's just class, the fans are nutty for it - they don't stop singing.

"There are a couple of British players in the team as well. Then after the match, win, lose or draw - one of the stands turns into a nightclub. I have never seen anything like it.

"It's open til five in the morning and a lot goes. Book your flight from wherever and watch them."

The nightclub, which is hosted by company KVO Arena Events, is a hugely popular venue which can also be used as a business centre.

But the main hall is the big attraction for visitors, which is known as the Spectacle Hall.

The firm describes it as: "The jewel in the crown of the Diaz Arena is undoubtedly the Spectacle Hall.

“A multifunctional room with mezzanine that offers you a wide range of possibilities.

"The hall has already been the setting for numerous fairs, galas, concerts, product presentations, staff parties and seminars.

“The flexible layout of the room also offers the necessary options for smaller parties and events."

KVO is currently 14th in the Jupiler Pro League, just four points above the relegation zone.

Their team currently includes Zech Medley, who came up through Arsenal’s youth side, and former Manchester City ace Thierry Ambrose.

Goalkeeper Dillon Phillips is also on loan to the side from Cardiff City.
