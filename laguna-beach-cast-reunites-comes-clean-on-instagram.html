Laguna Beach Cast Reunites, Comes Clean on Instagram

E! News is getting the Laguna Beach band back together.

A dozen years after this reality show premiered on MTV, various members of its beloved cast have reunited for a catch-up special that airs tonight at 7 p.m. and 11 p.m.

As pictured above on Instagram, Alex Murrel, Morgan Smith (née Olsen), Christina Schuller, Taylor Cole and Jason Wahler all gathered recently to relive their glory days on the cable network.

Murrel – who is currently pregnant and expecting a baby boy with her husband, Mark Johnson – shared this photo on her account, writing as a caption:

“Let’s go back … Back to the beginning."

This of a reference, of course, to the iconic Laguna Beach theme song, “Come Clean” by Hilary Duff.

In a follow-up post, meanwhile, Murrel also shared a selfie with her close pal Cole.

Here it is:

“YES we are still best friends Catch up with all of us tonight on @enews to hear secrets, stories and behind the scenes of Laguna Beach!” she wrote, adding:

“#LagunaBeachReunion #PartOne.”

The veteran friends appeared together on the show’s second season.

Sadly, neither Lauren Conrad nor Kristin Cavallari will take part in the reunion.

We can’t speak on the exact reasons why not, but Cavallari did recently say she hated her time on Laguna Beach. So that could explain it.

Laguna Beach ran from 2004 through 2006 on MTV and paved the way for The Hills.
