Josh Dun Biography

American drummer, Josh Dun is the famous musician known as one-half of the famous duo-driven musical band Twenty One Pilots. He also served as the live drummer for House of Heroes for several months.

Josh was interested in music since his childhood, but his parents didn't allow him to spend so long-time listening to music. However, he used to secretly sneak out and visit a music store. He used to collect large numbers of the music of his choice.
