Francesca Eastwood and Jordan Feldstein Annulment

After a week of being married, Francesca Eastwood, 20, and Jordan Feldstein, 35, have decided that an annulment is the best course of action.

Sources close to the situation say that the couple wed on Sunday, November 17. They reportedly regretted the alcohol fueled decision to wed in Sin City when they sobered up.

They picked up a marriage license at the Clark County Recorder’s Office and drove straight to the Simple Wedding Chapel to get hitched. For an added touch found only in Vegas — Elvis impersonators were available to serve as witnesses.

The day after TMZ announced the wedding and provided proof with a copy of the marriage certificate, Francesca was photographed at a high profile Los Angeles event without a wedding ring.

Feldstein is the brother of actor Jonah Hill and runs a successful management company that oversees the careers of some top music acts, including Maroon 5 and Adam Thicke.

Clint Eastwood’s daughter Francesca was spotted sneaking out of Mastro’s Steakhouse in Beverly Hills with a friend who is definitely NOT Feldstein.

Easy come, easy go! This almost makes Kim Kardashian’s 72-day marriage seem like a long-term gig…
