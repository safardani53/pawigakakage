Kerry Katona takes daughter Molly, 17, to weight loss boot camp in Greece

KERRY Katona is tackling her latest fitness feat alongside her eldest daughter Molly McFadden, 17, at the G.I. Jane Bootcamp in Greece.

The 38-year-old has been sharing a number of snaps from the residential health and fitness retreat based on the Greek Island of Ios since arriving six days ago.

The pair have been working up a sweat while taking part in water aerobics, hill walks, boxing classes, spinning sessions and Zumba.

Earlier in the week, Molly posted an animated selfie with Kerry after their morning hike up a mountain and wrote: "We’ve only gone and climbed a mountain".

Meanwhile, Kerry shared a selfie with Molly from their luxury resort by Mylopotas beach and thanked the fitness company for a great week.

Beaming for the sunset shot, the former Atomic Kitten star wrote: "@123_mollymc loved every minute being here @g.i.jane_bootcamp with my baby girl".

While Molly discussed her own experience at the retreat on Instagram and said she was "proud" of her journey over the past six days.

She captioned a photo of her at the top of hill: "... no but Seriously I am proud of myself for what I've done so far and I couldn't have done it without @g.i.jane_bootcamp

"Day 3 let's go (already my entire body aches good luck getting me up in the morning)".

Meanwhile, Kerry has insisted she is "happy" with her appearance despite putting on some weight recently.

The TV personality shared a picture of her pulling at her stomach, while on the Greek island, wearing a leopard print bikini.

She captioned the Instagram post: "So some pics have popped up online of me!!! Yes I have put some weight on but am happy!! I’m proud of my body!! I might not look sexy but I felt it!!

"The main thing is my head is in the best place it’s been in years and that’s what matters!!"

Kerry - who has admitted to being a yo yo dieter over the years - has admitted she has had "ups and downs", but decided to go on the fitness retreat to keep her healthy lifestyle on track and maintain a positive state of mind.

She continued: "I'm at bootcamp @g.i.jane_bootcamp to give me a kick up the backside and to keep on track of my state of mind!!

"We all have ups and downs it’s how we deal with those downs that show our strength!!!"

The refreshingly honest star admitted that she has made a "career" out of her fluctuating weight.

She pocketed £250k from her fitness DVD and was once paid £30k to lose several stone in eight weeks.
