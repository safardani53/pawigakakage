See Photos Of The Reality Star Hollywood Life

See photos of former ‘Real Housewives of Beverly Hills’s star Lisa Vanderpump, at events, out and about, and more over the years.

Lisa Vanderpump is a famous reality star who rose to fame as an original cast member on ‘The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills’. She was on the show for the first nine seasons until she had a falling out with all of her castmates over ‘puppygate.’ Co-star Dorit Kemsley had adopted a dog named Lucy from Lisa’s animal shelter, but gave it to a friend after it allegedly bit her child and husband. Somehow it ended up in a kill shelter, according to LVP, and the dog was returned to Lisa’s foundation.

The controversy of how Dorit’s dog ended up back at Lisa’s shelter ended up causing a feud between LVP and the rest of her cast, resulting in Lisa filming most of season nine separately from her co-stars. It also resulted in the end of her longtime friendship with fellow ‘RHOBH’ OG cast member Kyle Richards, who confronted Lisa on behalf of the cast to find out of she leaked the ‘puppygate’ story to Radar Online. It resulted in LVP’s husband Ken Todd kicking Kyle out of their home with the now infamous line delivered with such flourish, “Goodbye, Kyle!” Since then LVP’s castmates — including Kyle — have gone on to mock the line.

Lisa left RHOBH, but still stars on the fellow Bravo series, ‘Vanderpump Rules.’ Keep scrolling through the gallery for more photos of Lisa.
