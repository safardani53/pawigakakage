Anna Camp &amp; Skylar Astin Married Wedding Details For Pitch Perfect Co-Stars Hollywood

Wedding bells are ringing and a cappella groups are singing! Pitch Perfect co-stars and longtime lovers Anna Camp, 33, and Skylar Astin, 28, are now blissfully married, People reports.

The pair walked down the aisle together as man and wife on Sept. 10 in a private ceremony that took place outside in central California. Anna walked down the aisle in a Reem Acra dress, Vince Camuto shoes and Neil Lane jewels, while he wore Ermenegildo Zegna and Aldo shoes.

For those of you who happen to be major Pitch Perfect fans, you will be thrilled to know that their longtime friends and fellow Pitch Perfect co-stars, Brittany Snow and Ben Platt, were a part of their wedding party! They also had Kelley Jakle, 27, at their joint bachelor/ette party on Saturday, August 13. Isn’t it cute that the PP cast are all so close? We love it!

In June 2016, HollywoodLife.com EXCLUSIVELY spoke to Skylar about his and Anna’s wedding plans. “It’s going to be classy, romantic, and beautiful,” Skylar dished, painting a picture of how their wedding would be. “Just a wonderful celebration. And afterwards I think we’re going to plan a really fun after party for those who want to stay up a little later.” That sounds so awesome!

Anna and Skylar first announced that they were engaged in January 2016, almost three years after they started dating in June 2013. They met while filming the hilarious movie Pitch Perfect, and they have been inseparable ever since.
