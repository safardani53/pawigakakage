I have F-cup boobs I hate wearing bras and people always make the same cruel comments, I dont

A WOMAN with natural F cups has shared the cruel comments she often receives when she doesn’t wear a bra.

The busty fashion influencer isn't letting the snide remarks stop her from being comfortable while embracing her large chest.

Casee Brim (@caseebrim) is a self-proclaimed “bigger busted fairy godmother” who often posts outfit ideas for women with larger breasts and specifically for those who don’t like wearing bras.

In one TikTok, the body positive woman shared comments and questions that she often gets when she goes out without a bra on.

Wearing a yellow, cowl neck blouse draped off her shoulder, Casee confidently posed, clearly without a bra on underneath.

Over the video, she added text of what people have said to her.

“You need to wear a bra in public,” one of the comments read.

Another statement warned Casee: “Someone will see your nips.”

“They’re too big not to wear a bra,” someone has also said to her, pointing out her breast size.

Casee was also asked: "Why aren't you wearing a bra?"

But the largest piece of text over the video read: “Ur bewbs will sag if you don’t wear a bra.”

During all of this, Casee lip-synced along to Billy Joel singing: “I don’t care what you say anymore, this is my life.”

At the end, she flipped her middle finger to the camera, annoyed by the sexist and body-shaming comments.

“Comfort over everything else,” she wrote in the caption.

“I actually found my natural F cups became perkier when I started ‘training them’ to go bra free,” Casee said.

People commented in support and praise of the influencer.

“The confidence! I wanna be you!” someone wrote, and another added: “Finally someone I can relate to.”

“Bras are archaic and I don’t care what anyone says. Lmao I have D cups. I’m not wearing a bra,” someone else commented.

Others backed-up Casee’s claim that going bra-free made her breasts perkier and how some bras can promote sagging.

“They actually sag less as they have to grow the strength to hold themselves up,” one person agreed about the pros of going bra-free.

In a similar TikTok, Casee shared that she gives outfit inspo to bigger-busted women who feel like they can only wear baggy clothes.

In it, she wore a slightly see-through corset top, which showed that she wasn’t wearing a bra underneath.

And in another video, she shared that people have also said to her: “If you don’t start dressing more modestly, I’m going to unfollow you.”

Instead of succumbing to peer pressure, Casee posed in a fitted dress with a plunging neckline that showed off her cleavage, sans bra.

“There’s the door. Wear whatever the heck makes you feel good, girl!” she wrote in the caption.
