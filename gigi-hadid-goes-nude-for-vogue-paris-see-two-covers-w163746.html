Gigi Hadid Goes Nude for 'Vogue Paris': See Two Covers

https://www.instagram.com/p/BBiTEemDCaK/

When in Rome — er, make that Paris! Gigi Hadid landed not one but two Vogue Paris covers, and she's completely naked in one of them.

PHOTOS: Gigi and Bella Hadid: Model Sisters

"What an honor and dream come true it is to be on the cover of French Vogue," Hadid, 20, wrote via Instagram of her March 2016 issue. "Thank you so much [photographers] @mertalas [and editor-in-chief] @emmanuellealt."

That was for the fully clothed version — or at least the take with a jacket that covered the essentials. The second cover features Hadid, who is currently dating Zayn Malik, cross-legged and wearing nothing but shoes and bracelets. "And here's the version where I'm wearing mostly Chanel N°5," she wrote, adding a winking face.

Her mom, Yolanda Foster, was especially proud. "Social judgement can break down a young woman temporarily but a real WOMAN will always pick up the pieces, rebuild herself and come back stronger than ever," she wrote via Instagram. "So proud of you my love for owning your authenticity on the cover of FRENCH VOGUE with class and grace."

Her best friend Kendall Jenner's half-sister Khloe Kardashian went so far as to call Hadid her "spirit animal." "Epic f—king cover!!!! Bawwwwdy for days!!" she wrote. "Lezzzzz-be-honest…. I'm in love."
