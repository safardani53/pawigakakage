Catherine Giudici Shows Pre-Wedding Bikini Body in Miami: Pictures

When the Bachelor’s away, the Bachelorettes will play! Sean Lowe‘s fiancee, Catherine Giudici, enjoyed a sunny getaway with more than a dozen of her closest friends in Miami, Fla., this past weekend. While there, she showed off her sexy pre-wedding bikini body in a couple of colorful two-piece swimsuits.

On Monday, Dec. 9, the 27-year-old graphic designer rocked an asymmetrical black bandeau top with a single, diagonally placed strap. She paired the top with a pair of bright coral bikini bottoms — and her diamond engagement ring from Lowe, whose heart she stole on season 17 of ABC’s The Bachelor.

On another day during the trip, the blushing bride-to-be flaunted her curves in a neon yellow halter top and skimpy side-tie bottoms. She appeared to be in a great mood as she talked and laughed with friends.

“Life is fun,” she tweeted on Sunday, Dec. 8. “I am lucky to have the coolest chicks to call my friends!”

Judging by Giudici’s Twitter, the group’s getaway was a busy one. On Saturday, she tweeted that she was taking part in “a little water workout with the women” — paddle-boarding and kayaking. Later, she shared a picture of the pals all dressed up for dinner at Katsuya, where they ate sashimi and sushi and enjoyed “Kiwi Envy” cocktails.

From sunny Miami, the reality TV alum headed up to chilly New York, where she met up with her fiance. The couple helped donate coats to people in need at Manhattan’s Bryant Park on Dec. 11.
