Hot Pics Hollywood Life

Coachella is back for the 2023 season, and in honor of the event, we’re reminiscing about some of the hottest looks we’ve seen stars wear while attending in years past. One hot Coachella trend has always been the bikini. After all, it’s HOT in the desert where the music festival takes place, so bikinis have doubled as clothing for plenty of stars over the years.

In addition to stars wearing bikinis at the concert portion of Coachella, we’ve also seen them wearing swimsuits to attend parties that take place on the festival grounds. Coachella pool parties are the perfect place to break out a two-piece, which can then also be worn to check out the live music at night. Stars like Vanessa Hudgens, Alessandra Ambrosio, Halsey, and more have all slayed in bikinis at Coachella over the years.

Vanessa Hudgens

Vanessa rocked this animal print triangle bikini top with low-rise denim shorts, a thick leather belt, jewelry, and a black floppy hat at Coachella.

Let’s be real – Vanessa is one of the ultimate Coachella queens. Every year when she goes to the festival, she rocks the coolest style. Here, she pairs a tiny pink Triangl bikini top, along with a pair of high-waisted hot pink sheer pants and a flowy silk shirt. She also wore a patterned bucket hat to complete her desert attire.

Alessandra Ambrosio

Alessandra always knows how to slay a bikini, and she did just that while attending a Coachella party one year. The model paired a strapless bikini top with ripped jeans and heeled sandals. She also rocked a cross-body bag and round-shaped sunglasses for her summery ensemble. Alessandra was all smiles as she strolled through the Coachella grounds in the sexy look.
