I'm a car expert... there's a hidden feature which starts your motor instantly

A CAR expert has revealed a little known way to start a car's motor without even being in the vehicle.

The salesman, from Gormley Car Sales in Maghera, Northern Ireland, detailed the trick in a Tiktok that's already garnered 400,000 views.

In the clip, the creator demonstrates the move on a Ford Fiesta and challenges that his viewers "didn't know this" about their car.

He then moved the camera to prove no one was in the vehicle.

"You can see there's nobody sitting inside this car, as you can see, not a person inside that car," he said.

The man produced the Ford's key fob from his pocket to start the demonstration.
