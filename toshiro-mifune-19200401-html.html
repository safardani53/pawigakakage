Toshiro Mifune (Movie Actor) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Toshiro Mifune is a Japanese actor who has appeared in more than 150 feature films. He is known for his 16 collaborations with Akira Kurosawa in works such as Rashomon, Seven Samurai, The Hidden Fortress, Throne of Blood, and Yojimbo. He also played Miyamoto Musashi in Hiroshi Inagaki's Samurai trilogy and another Inagaki series, Lord Toranaga in NBC's Shōgun miniseries and Admiral Isoroku Yamamoto in three different films.
