Manchester United to wear their away blue kit in Europa League final against Ajax

MANCHESTER UNITED will play the Europa League final in their blue kit.

Ajax have been drawn as the home team for the Stockholm clash on May 24.

And that means that the Dutch giants will be in their iconic red and white for the final.

Meanwhile United will be in all-blue as they seek to secure another trophy and Champions League action for next season.

History bodes well for Mourinho's men in blue.

They triumphed in their first European final back in 1968 wearing blue.

Bobby Charlton netted a double as United downed Benfica at Wembley.

United boss Jose Mourinho is set to play the kids this weekend to rest his stars for the Ajax clash.

And he insists that they will head to Stockholm in good spirits, with all their injured stars in the stands.

Mourinho said: "Everybody goes. We have a few on crutches but everybody goes.

"A fantastic group of players with fantastic spirit. A difficult run and lots of matches.

"We spent more time travelling and in hotels than at home because 60-something matches means 140 days in hotels.

"So half the year we spent together and that's only possible when the spirit is amazing.

"So everyone is going to be there.  A few we have on crutches. Ashley Young for sure, Marcos Rojo, Zlatan Ibrahimovic. But we all go together."
