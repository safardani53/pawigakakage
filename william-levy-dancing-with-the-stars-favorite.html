Dancing With the Stars Favorite?

Who is William Levy?

If you asked that heading into this season of Dancing With the Stars, you aren’t alone. But if he keeps this up, fewer and fewer people will be in the dark.

The ballroom screaming for the Cuban soap star, a.k.a. The Brad Pitt of Cuba, is certainly an encouraging sign for his hopes of hoisting the Mirror Ball.

His scores aren’t bad either.

Levy scored a 25 for his Quickstep, which put him in the #2 spot on the leaderboard behind Katherine Jenkins and Roshon Fegan, who both earned 26s.

But in terms of crowd support and catcalls, he was alone at the top.

“I have no words to explain how good it feels,” Levy told reporters. “When I was a little boy in Cuba, my dream was to come to America, like 3 years old. Today to be at this position in my life to receive this kind of love in this country, it’s amazing. It’s crazy.”

Think he’s got what it takes to win the thing?
