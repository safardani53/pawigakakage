Adam Levine and Behati Prinsloos Relationship Timeline

Adam Levine and Behati Prinsloo are a match made in Victoria’s Secret heaven — but their romance wasn’t always easy.

The Maroon 5 singer began dating Prinsloo in May 2012, shortly after breaking up with fellow Victoria’s Secret Angel Anne Vyalitsyna one month prior. Although Levine and Prinsloo briefly broke up in May 2013, they couldn’t deny their connection, rekindling their romance less than two months later.

Us Weekly exclusively broke the news in July 2014 that the pair tied the knot and Levine and Prinsloo soon began expanding their family with daughters Dusty Rose and Gio Grace. “He’s actually stricter than me and I thought I was going to be the strict one,” Prinsloo exclusively told Us in December 2018. “He’s a very hands-on dad. It’s awesome. I’m lucky.”

After announcing in September 2022 that they were expecting baby No. 3, Levine and Prinsloo’s relationship took a hit after the singer was accused of having a year-long affair with an Instagram model.
