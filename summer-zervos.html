Summer Zervos Celebrity Bio Hollywood Life

Bio:
Summer Zervos (born in Huntington Beach, California) is an American businesswoman and restaurant owner. She competed on season five of Donald Trump‘s The Apprentice show, but was the first contestant fired that round. Summer later met with Donald to discuss further employment opportunities in 2007. Over their business lunch at The Beverly Hills Hotel, Donald allegedly “thrust his genitals” against her, and began kissing her and “grabbing her breasts.” Summer came forward with these accusations on Oct. 14, 2016.

Best Known For:
Summer is best known for coming out as one of Donald’s sexual assault accusers.

Personal Life:
Despite having zero restauranteur experience, the Orange County-native managed to work her way up the ladder of success very quickly She started off working for minimum wage, and it wasn’t long before she was the owner of Sunny’s Restaurant in California. She is said to be the 11th woman to come forward to accuse Donald of sexual assault. She’s believed to be single.
