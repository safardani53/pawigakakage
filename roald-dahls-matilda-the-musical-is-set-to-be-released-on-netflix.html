Roald Dahls Matilda the Musical Is Set To Be Released On Netflix

Roald Dahl’s Matilda the Musical, Netflix’s next musical film, is set to premiere on the streaming site on Sunday, December 25, 2022, at 3 a.m. ET. Based on Roald Dahl’s book Matilda, the film depicts the narrative of Matilda, a highly clever girl who is not afraid to make big choices. Alisha Weiras Matilda stars in the picture, with many others playing significant supporting parts. The film is directed by Matthew Warchus and written by Dennis Kelly. It was released in cinemas on December 9, 2022, to positive reviews.

Matilda the Musical by Roald Dahl on Netflix: Plot, trailer, what to anticipate, and more topics discussed

On June 15, 2022, Netflix published the official teaser for Roald Dahl’s Matilda the Musical. The teaser provides a glimpse into the fascinating tale, which includes many interesting characters. It begins with a view of a school’s gate, followed by a violent voiceover saying:

“This school is rife with rebels.”

The video then introduces the main heroine, Matilda Wormwood, who is portrayed to be a brave and intelligent young woman who is not afraid to challenge authority. It also shows some of the dance routines done by the students at the school. The movie’s description on Netflix’s Tudum is as follows:

“A remarkable child with a bright intellect and a creative imagination makes a risky decision to modify her tale – with amazing outcomes.”

According to the teaser and description, viewers can expect an exciting and adventurous musical drama centered on an enormously charming figure who achieves marvels. It also has a lot of amusing and uplifting moments that might make for an ideal vacation experience. Matilda the Musical is the second cinematic adaptation of Roald Dahl’s popular book. The first picture, simply named Matilda, was released in 1996 and got rave reviews for its delightful tone, tonal consistency, and cast performances.

A peek at the cast of Roald Dahl’s Matilda the Musical.

Matilda Wormwood, the protagonist of the forthcoming musical film, is played by young actress Alisha Weir. Alisha looks great in the film, and she effortlessly reflects her character’s aggressive and courageous attributes. Fans may look forward to a pleasant performance from this young talent. Alisha has been in Michael Tully’s Don’t Leave Home in addition to Roald Dahl’s Matilda the Musical. She also provided the voice of Primrose in Two by Two: Overboard!.

Lashana Lynch, who portrays Miss Jennifer Honey in the forthcoming Netflix musical film, is another important cast member. Lynch looks stunning in the trailer and promises to offer an enthralling performance. Her other cinematic acting credits include mentioning a few, No Time to Die, Captain Marvel, and Still Star-Crossed.

Aside from Alisha and Lashana, the film also contains a number of notable performers in key supporting parts. Emma Thompson plays Miss Agatha Trunchbull, Andrea Riseborough plays Mrs. Wormwood, and Stephen Graham plays Mr. Harry Wormwood, among others. Roald Dahl’s Matilda the Musical will be available on Netflix on Sunday, December 25, 2022, at 3 a.m. ET.
