Jon and Kate Gosselin's daughter Mady, 19, twerks in TikTok dance with sister Alexis, 16, as family

JON and Kate Gosselin's daughter MADY shared a rare new video of herself twerking with her sister Alexis amid their family's bitter feud.

Mady, 19, uploaded the fun clip of herself and 16-year-old Alexis to TikTok after discovering it hidden in her drafts folder.

In the footage, the siblings are all smiles as they dance along to Brigit Mendler's 2013 hit song Hurricane.

Alexis is first to appear in shot, peering into view before quickly backing off again.

Mady then bursts onto the screen, twerking with her leg in the air as she clutches an orange juice in one hand.

The girls' dog Shoka, who at one point was also embroiled in the Gosselins' family feud, clearly doesn't want to miss out on the fun and joins in too.
