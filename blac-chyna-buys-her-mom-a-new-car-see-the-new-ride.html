Blac Chyna Buys Her Mom a New Car, Toni Cries With Joy

Blac Chyna is living large, and she’s sharing the wealth with her mom.

The model recently bought her mother, Tokyo Toni (real name Shalana Jones Hunter), a brand new Nissan 2016, and both women are ecstatic.

Blac shared a video of the blessed event to Instagram.

"Today was an amazing day!" Blac wrote in the caption. "Got my Mom a fully loaded 2016 Nissan, Exactly what she asked for!"

"She’s not a flashy person and it makes me so Happy that I was able to do this for her! LOVE YOU MOM!!" she added.

In the vid, Toni jumps up and down with excitement, screaming, "Chyna got me a car!"

The two then check out the ride and Blac tells her it’s fully loaded with a double sunroof and leather seats.

Toni shared a number of photos and videos to her own Instagram account, thanking her daughter and taking pics with Blac and Rob Kardashian, who was also present.
