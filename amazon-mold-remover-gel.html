GEL MAGIC

The gel cleaner is unscented and comes with four fluid ounces of product.

It sticks to any surface and won't run down inclined surfaces.

It comes with a removable nozzle tip, so you can choose the amount of product you want to administer.

HOW IT WORKS

Using this cleaning hack to kill mold is easy.

Simply dry the affected area and apply the gel, letting it sit for five to 10 hours.

Just note that the longer you let the product sit, the more effective it is at eliminating mold.

Once the product has sat, rinse it off with water or wipe the residue with a towel.

Use gloves when using the product to avoid getting it on your hands.

For areas with greater amounts of mold, you can use more letting it sit longer, or you can repeat the previous steps multiple times.
