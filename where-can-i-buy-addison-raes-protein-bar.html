Where can I buy Addison Rae's protein bar?

TIKTOK icon Addison Rae drops a collab with Alani Nu, a better-for-you health and wellness brand.

The partnership includes a new Alani Nu protein bar flavor and an exciting energy drink flavor.

Who is Addison Rae?

Addison Rae, born October 6, 2000, is the fourth most followed content creator on TikTok, with 86.6million followers.

Addison started posting on TikTok back in July 2019 and quickly rose to fame.

By July 3, 2021, she surpassed 81million followers on TikTok.

Aside from TikTok, the social media personality debuted with her first single Obsessed in March 2021.

Where can I buy Addison Rae's protein bar?

Addison Rae and Alaina Nu dropped two new items for the collaboration, including Cookie Dough protein bar and Berry Pop energy drink.

Addison Rae's collaboration with Alani Nu can be purchased on Alani Nu's official website and Amazon.

Products are also available offline in certain Target, Kroger, Vitamin Shoppe, and GNC stores.

The 16g protein Cookie Dough protein bar has just 4g of sugar and is advertised as healthier, even with its candy-like goodness. Berry Pop contains 200mg of caffeine, no sugar, B vitamins, and biotin, perfect for a fresh retreat.

Per 12-pack both the Berry Pop energy drink and Cookie Dough protein bar will retail for $29.99.

This collaboration also offers an exclusive Rae Family Bundle, which includes 12 Berry Pop Energy Drinks, 12 Cookie Dough Protein Bars, and limited-edition merch.

The special bundle is $59.98.

What is Alani Nu?

Alani Nu was founded in 2018 by Katy Hearn, a fitness influencer from Maryland.

The brand focuses on creating healthier, low-calorie products for customers that taste yummy. Alani Nu is active on social media, including Instagram and Twitter.
