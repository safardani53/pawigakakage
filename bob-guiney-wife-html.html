Bob Guiney's Married Life with Third Wife Has Been Adventurous and Rewarding So Far

Bob Guiney is a reality TV personality best known for The Bachelor franchise. He gained popularity from the first season of The Bachelorette and later became the bachelor on The Bachelor season 4. 

Regardless of being on two dating shows, Guiney ended up alone. He was married for a short time before and after the show — both ending in divorce. 

After two unsuccessful marriages, it seems Guiney has found his happily ever after with his third wife, Jessica Canyon. They have been together for over six years now and are living their best lives.

Bob Guiney's Third Wife and Children 

Not much is known about Canyon and her dating timeline with the reality star. The pair got engaged in October 2015, and a little over a year later, they said their “I dos.”

On November 11, 2016, the couple got married on the occasion of Guiney’s father’s birthday. The wedding ceremony was held at a luxurious beachfront villa, Casa Entre Rocas, in Punta Mita, Mexico.

Two years after his marriage, Guiney became a dad for the first time. The duo welcomed their first child, Grayson Robert, on December 3, 2018. 

Canyon took to Instagram to share that she had prolonged labor while giving birth to her oldest son.

Grayson was born after 24 hours of contractions, followed by four hours of pushing.

In the end, he was delivered by cesarean section. 

Recently, The Bachelor alum announced the birth of their second child on his Instagram. They welcomed baby Blake Barrett on July 1, 2021. 

Given how often the pair posts about their parenthood odyssey, perhaps no one is as happy as Canyon and Guiney to be parents.

From feeding their sons to playing with them, they share it all with their fans and followers. Canyon regards her husband as the “best dad,” while Guiney considers himself the “luckiest Dad out there.”

Guiney's Ex-Wives 

Before Guiney starred in The Bachelor franchise, he was married to Jennifer Lantz. They two dated when they were at Michigan State University and got married soon after. 

They separated after more than two years of marriage and eventually divorced. 

Guiney then starred in The Bachelorette in 2003, where he was rejected by Trista Sutter for Ryan Sutter. Regardless, he is good friends with both of them to this day.

In 2004, the reality TV star was the bachelor and chose Estella Gardinier amongst 25 single ladies. 

However, their relationship could not last. Guiney did not propose to Gardinier, and shortly after the show aired, the two went different ways. 

Guiney’s second marriage was to actress and TV presenter Rebecca Budig. She is best known for All My Children, The Better Show, and General Hospital. 

It was reported that the pair started dating in November 2003 while Guiney was supposedly with Gardinier. Guiney and Budig got engaged on May 20, 2004. 

Just after a month of engagement, the duo was married in July 2004. The wedding ceremony was held at Guiney’s family home in Michigan by an idyllic lake. 

The marriage was low-key, with only 50 guests attending the ceremony. The marriage lasted for almost six years before the couple separated. 

Budig filed for divorce in 2010 as they were growing apart. Both of them had realized that there was nothing they could do to save their marriage.

After the divorce was finalized, Guiney was a bachelor again for the next six years until he met the love of his life — Canyon.
