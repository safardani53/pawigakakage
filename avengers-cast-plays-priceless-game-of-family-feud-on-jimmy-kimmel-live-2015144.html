Avengers Cast Plays Priceless Game of Family Feud on Jimmy Kimmel Live

A feud for the ages! The stars of The Avengers mega-franchise stopped by Jimmy Kimmel Live! on Monday, April 13, and competed in an old-fashioned round of Family Feud. 

Promoting the upcoming Avengers: Age of Ultron, Scarlett Johansson, Robert Downey Jr., Chris Hemsworth, Chris Evans, Jeremy Renner, and Mark Ruffalo were split into two teams of three by host Jimmy Kimmel.

“Let’s meet our teams!” the funnyman began. “First, led by Captain America Chris Evans, the Americas!” Evans, 33, was joined by Renner, who plays Hawkeye, and Ruffalo, The Hulk.

“Their opponents, led by Iron Man himself, Robert Downey Jr., The Mans!” Kimmel continued, introducing the other team of Downey Jr., Hemsworth (Thor) and Johansson (Black Widow). “Let’s play the Feud!”

Evans and Downey Jr., 50, kicked off the game, facing off against one another in the first round. “We have the top four answers on the board,” Kimmel explained. “We asked 100 people, ‘Name something you do in a movie theater besides watching a movie?'”

Downey Jr. buzzed in first, prompting Evans to exclaim, “Outrageous!” The teams went back and forth, giving answers of “eat popcorn,” “use your mobile device,” and Ruffalo’s unsuccessful guess of “sleep.” Hemsworth, 31, took it home for The Mans, giving the winning answer of “make out.” 

The Australian hunk stepped into the spotlight for round two, competing against Ruffalo with a question of, “Besides superheroes, name a profession that requires you to wear tights.” Ruffalo buzzed in first, answering quickly, “Trapeze artist.”

While his teammates and even host Kimmel laughed at his answer, Ruffalo got the number two choice with his unexpected guess. It wasn’t enough however, as he was beat out by Hemsworth’s answer of simply, “Ballet”
