Felipe Caicedo Biography

Felipe Caicedo is not married yet, however, he is involved in love affairs with his girlfriend, Maria Garcia. They began dating each other from the start of 2006 and also announced once that they were about to get married and be each other’s husband and wife. He is not divorced but he claims that he is in no hurry to be his girlfriend's loving spouse.

 Felipe Caicedo dreamed of football since his early days. He was just fifteen years old when he first played his club games but at that time he played from the youth team as the youth player. He made his senior team appearance in 2006 from Basel. He remained there for two years scoring 11 goals out of his 45 appearances. In 2008, he joined Manchester United rival Manchester City. He remained there for three years but his professional life there was not as good as he expected. He now plays from Espanyol and has also scored ten goals from there. He has also won international caps from the Ecuadorian national team. He is earning a massive salary being a professional player and his earnings for 2014 was around a net worth of 3 million EUR.
