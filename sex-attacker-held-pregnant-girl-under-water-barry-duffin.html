Teen sex attacker 'held pregnant girlfriend's head under water after she stopped him attending baby

A TWISTED teen predator is alleged to have held his pregnant girlfriend's head under water after she stopped him attending baby scan.

Barry Duffin, 18, was jailed for four and a half years for abusing the girl, then 15, and two others, aged just 13 and 14.

The teen – now 16 – has now said she thought she was “going to die” during one of his vile attacks.

She told the Daily Record: “I had a scan coming up. I didn’t let him go.

“He dragged me to the toilet. He grabbed my hair and forced my head into the bath."

She added: “He held me under the water and he was spraying me in the face with the shower.

“He used to tell me to beg for my life almost every time we fought.”

Duffin, from Paisley, Scotland, was branded a danger to women in court this week after it was revealed his youngest victim was just 13 years and two months when he slept with her at a home in Paisley.

He was jailed at Paisley Sheriff Court for sleeping with the three youngsters.

His victim – who cannot be named for legal reasons – said she met the bully through friends.

But he soon started ‘cutting her off’ from family. She also found out he had cheated on her.

Sheriff David Pender told how the pervert “wore” the girls down until they “felt obliged” to fulfil his demands.

He added: “The public, girls and young women in particular require to be protected from you.”

Duffin repeatedly abused a 14-year-old between December 2014 and January 2015 when he was two and a half years older than her.

He was barred from contacting any of his three victims and placed him on the Sex Offenders’ Register.
