Mariah pulls all the stops at Christmas

Mariah Carey began the six-night residency "All I Want for Christmas Is You: A Night of Joy and Festivity" at New York's Beacon Theatre in 2014.

In the two years that followed, she brought back the residency at Beacon before extending it into a tour, taking the spectacular to Europe.

She finished it at MSG in 2019 following a series of concerts in Las Vegas, Atlantic City, and Boston.
