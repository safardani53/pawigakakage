Mariah Carey's $125,000 a Week Hamptons Hideaway - Stone Meadow Farm

Mariah Carey and hubby Nick Cannon are finally getting around to celebrating their April nuptials and after a lavish party hosted by Def Jam boss L.A. Reid, the couple will retreat to swanky Stone Meadow Farm in East Hampton, NY, for a little R&R.

Shelling out $125,000 to rent the 18,000 square foot, 8-acre digs for a week (yes, that’s right kiddies, I said $125,000 a week), the divalicious couple plan to “chill out.”

With 10 bedrooms, 12.5 bathrooms, a gym, swimming pool, tennis court, a stable, fenced riding fields and two separate guest houses, Mr. and Mrs. Mariah will have plenty of room for chilling.

Mariah currently owns a condo in TriBeca and a home in the Bahamas. Should she opt to purchase her Hamptons hideaway, it is currently on the market for just under $20 million.
