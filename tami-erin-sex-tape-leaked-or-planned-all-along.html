Leaked or Planned All Along?

Pippi Longstocking star Tami Erin insisted again this week that she’s only releasing her sex tape to beat her bitter ex to the punch. Heard that one before?

Earlier this summer, the Tami Erin sex tape was being shopped to various media outlets, which she claimed was news to her and she would fight all the way.

A few weeks later? So much for that.

Much like Farrah Abraham and Kim Kardashian, who used the same line and got very rich after their sex tapes hit the market, Erin had to be in on this.

It’s just a question of when.

 

She claims she’s just a victim trying to get ahead of a bad situation with an ex-boyfriend, but she could easily put the kibosh on the situation if she wanted.

If her ex were shopping a tape and she truly wanted to stop it, she would more than likely succeed. But there would be no money in that, would there?

If she shot an amateur porno film with the intention of "leaking" it, that wouldn’t be nearly as interesting and would be even more shameless, wouldn’t it?

In any case, The New Adventures of Pippi Longstocking star is ready to release the tape to the highest bidder. But is anyone buying what she’s selling?

We mean both the tape, and her explanation. Discuss.
