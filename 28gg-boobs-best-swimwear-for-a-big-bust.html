Im a 28GG &amp; have found all the best swimwear for a big bust - theyre so flattering

A WOMAN with 28GG boobs has shared how she’s found the best swimwear items for this summer.

Jen Warnes has a tiny waist and bigger chest and often struggles to find fashionable items that flatter her figure. 

However, after searching the web for swimsuits, Jen said she’d found some stylish hidden gems – and couldn’t wait to get them off her chest.

In a video posted to her TikTok account @itsjenwarnes, the influencer modelled her finds one by one, before revealing where others could buy them from.

Jen explained: “Here are swimsuits I’ve found for a small band, large cup size.”

Up first was a simple but stylish black swimsuit with a small cut out in the waist and larger straps for support.

Jen continued to her 209k followers: “The first one is from Lusciousworld Swim called the World Disco One Piece.

“I’m wearing an extra small in this one – like how, I don’t know? 

“The straps at the back help cinch and lift you and there’s plenty of room for a larger cup size.”

The second was one of Jen’s personal favourites, as she stepped out in front of the camera in a plain black swimming costume with thinner straps.

This design can be bought from Andie Swimwear and is called their Amalfi One Piece.

Jen went on to say: “This one is probably my personal favourite and most worn.

“I love this one, it’s so comfortable, I wear it all the time.

“It has a built-in shelf bra for support and just hugs me really nicely.”

The next two on the list can both be purchased from a fuller-bust brand called All Peace and differ in design.

First, Jen flaunted her figure in a one piece that looks like a bikini with a white, one-shouldered top and black bottoms. This is called the Mombasa Asymmetric One Piece. 

She said she loved the look and also that it features “built in support.”

Meanwhile, the second style is once again a black swimming costume – but the Underwired One Piece comes complete with bra style cups.

“It fits you small enough in the body, but then the cups are very generous for a larger chest.”

The penultimate buy on Jen’s list is a Marvel Lane swimsuit – specifically their Sienna One Piece. 

She explained: “It comes with built-in cups but it comes in your bra size and has an adjustable hook at the back.
