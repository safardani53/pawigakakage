Where are Matt and Lauren from Married At First Sight now?

MARRIED At First Sight Australia has got everyone talking, and one couple that caught our attention was Lauren and Matthew.

The hit reality show was actually filmed back in 2018, so fans are keen to know where the former couple are now.

Where are Matt and Lauren from Married At First Sight now?

They started off well, and Lauren Huntriss even popped Matthew Bennett's cherry on their honeymoon, but things quickly turned sour.

Lauren confessed to her new husband that she liked "kinky" sex, and he told the fellow contestants he "wasn't attracted" to her.

They were then the first couple to leave the experiment.

As of 2021, Lauren continues to be a make-up artist and shares her styling tips on Instagram.

She also undergone an incredible weight transformation 

Lauren keeps her personal life away from Instagram so it is unclear whether she is single or taken. 

Meanwhile, Matt has underogone a major transformation of his own.

The 31-year-old videographer has been inundated with X-rated messages from fans since appearing on the show.

He even stripped down to his underwear for a raunchy photoshoot with famed Aussie photographer John Bortolin.

Matt has also been in a relationship with his girlfriend Annabel Jameson since 2019.

Where have Matt and Lauren said about MAFS?

Lauren hasn't held back in slamming the reality show that made her famous and even said it was "full of s**t."

In February 2021, the blunt star took to her Instagram page to suggest she was "pulled" off the street to feature on the show.

The same month Matt explained in a YouTube video that he'd developed a urinary tract infection early in the first week of the experiment.

He shockingly said that he was only allowed to go to the toilet "twice during the whole day" due to the complexity of shooting his wedding.
