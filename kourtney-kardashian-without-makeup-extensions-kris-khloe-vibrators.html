KOURTNEY Kardashian showed off her bare face and went makeup-free in a new video to dish on her favorite Valentine’s Day products.

The 42-year-old opened up about the products she sent her friends and family, just after her mom, Kris Jenner, revealed she got a vibrator from her daughter.

In a series of new videos on her Instagram Story, Kourtney gave her skin some rest and used a rejuvenating face mask as she directed her discussion at her fans.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum went without her hair extensions and her usual glam-grunge makeup, as she's often seen in while hanging with her fiancé Travis Barker.

She shared in the videos: “So, for Valentine’s Day, I love to send a gift to all my friends, my sisters, and my mom. 

“And I am gonna show you, I put together this Poosh Valentine’s Day mailer that I will be sending to my close friends, sisters, and mom. I’m gonna show you what it is.”

Kourtney continued: “So, we partnered with Clarins. We did these beautiful baskets from Potterybarn Kids because we thought they were so cute with the Valentine’s Day theme.

“So, I just poured the second whole basket all out on the floor.

EYE-OPENING GIFTS

On Wednesday, her mom Kris, 66, took to her own Instagram Story to show off the presents she received from Kourtney.

The series of videos began with Kris telling her eldest daughter that she and her lifestyle brands “really outdid” themselves with the presents.

Like Kourtney said, Kris revealed the gift set included Clarins creams and serums, an anti-aging face shield, SKIMS apparel, makeup and candles.

The gift took an interesting turn when the Kardashian matriarch revealed, though, that she also received “sex serum,” a vibrator, and a red lacy thong with pearl detailing.

Kris said off camera: “This is very sexy for Valentine’s Day.

“Thanks Kourtney! I love you. This is amazing.”

Khloe, 37, also did a box unveiling by showing off the same items she received from her older sister.
