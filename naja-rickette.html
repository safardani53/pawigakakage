Who is Naja Rickette:

Naja Rickette is a famous Reality Star. She was born on June 3, 1979 and her birthplace is Trenton, NJ. Naja is also well known as, Also known as “The Queen of Nails” or “The Naja,” she is a couture nail artist and stylist who is known for being the pioneer of Minx nails. She opened the Naja Nail Guru Nail Academy and became a television personality on the reality series L.A. Hair. She has also been the owner of LA-based salon Nail Spa Extremedies Hand & Foot Spa, which has had visits from Missy Elliott, Tamar Braxton and Lady Gaga.

Naja is originated from United States. She styled the nails of Fergie for the Black Eyed Peas music video of the song “Boom, Boom, Boom.” US Weekly named her work on client Miranda Lambert the Best Mani of The Grammys in 2013.
