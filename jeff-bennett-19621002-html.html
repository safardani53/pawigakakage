Jeff Bennett is an American voice actor. His vocal roles include Johnny Bravo in the TV series of the same name, Dexter's father in Dexter's lab, Petrie in The Land Before Time, The Man With the Yellow Hat in Curious George, Raj and Samson in Camp. Lazlo, Dr Jacques von Hämsterviel in the Lilo & Stitch series, Kowalski in The Penguins of Madagascar, Jonathan Long in American Dragon: Jake Long, Principal Pestrip in The Buzz on Maggie, and many other characters in films and television series, images and video games. In 2012, Bennett received the Annie Award for his role in Penguins of Madagascar and in 2016, he received the Emmy Award for his role in Transformers: Rescue Bots. He has been listed among the biggest names in dubbing.
