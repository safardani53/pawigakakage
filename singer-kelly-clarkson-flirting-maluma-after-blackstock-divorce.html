KELLY Clarkson has been caught flirting with the Colombian singer, Maluma, on the set of her television show.

"We're having an incredible time! Thank you all so much for your love and support. Truly, we're having a great time! Kisses and hugs always!" Maluma added.

Kelly's mouth was gaping open in a huge smile, as Maluma spoke quickly and flawlessly to her in Spanish.
