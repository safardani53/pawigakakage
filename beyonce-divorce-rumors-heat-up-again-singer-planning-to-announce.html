Singer Planning to Announce Jay Z Split?!

Beyonce is officially planning her divorce announcement from Jay Z after seven years of marriage, according to a bombshell new tabloid expose.

The cause of the billion-dollar breakup, according to this report?

Years of suspected infidelity on his part have allegedly pushed Beyonce to her breaking point, and she’s reportedly had it with the whole charade.

“It’s dead,” an insider told In Touch, the beacon of celeb journalism.

“Bey is sick of keeping up the ruse of happiness, and she’s done.”

Of course, we’ve heard all of this before, and not even that long ago following Solange’s elevator fight with Jay Z as Beyonce looked on stoically.

They weathered that, but sources say the “marriage is over” now:

“It’s like she’s on lockdown and doesn’t want to be asked about her marriage or why she and Jay haven’t had another child after Blue Ivy.”

“But in private, she’s already told friends and family that she’s moving forward with her life. She’ll soon make a public announcement.”

Rumors have swirled for years that Jay Z has been cheating on Queen Bey with celebrities such as Rihanna, Rachel Roy and Rita Ora.

None of those reports have been confirmed, for what it’s worth.

Still, the couple’s supposed decision to divorce comes weeks after they had a huge blowup leading up to the MTV VMAs. Why, you ask?
