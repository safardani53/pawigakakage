Sophia Bush Poses Topless: PHOTO

Oh hey, Amy Schumer. Looks like you’ve got a fan!

Former One Tree Hill star Sophia Bush was apparently so taken with the comedian’s nearly-nude Entertainment Weekly cover, she decided to replicate it, posing topless with nothing but the magazine itself.

Bush, 32, posted the racy pic on Instagram Tuesday, captioning it: “local single in your area @amyschumer call me.”

Now there’s a pair of ladies who could break the Internet.

Despite the plethora of liquor bottles, Schumer, 33, needed no liquid courage to pose for the shoot, a take on Mena Suvari‘s iconic shot in American Beauty. In fact, the Trainwreck actress came up with the idea herself!

“I was like, ‘Stop it – I’m going to be naked,’ ” she told EW.
