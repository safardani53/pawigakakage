A BEAUTY bargain hunter has made the ultimate lip product discovery at Dollar Tree.

She was more than pleased with the frugal find that was giving her pout the perfect long-lasting color she had been waiting for.

After watching a fellow TikToker trying the buy that instantly transformed her lips, she knew she had to get on the beauty bandwagon.

She even braved the pouring rain on her quest to find the elusive - but potentially life-changing lip item.

"That Dollar Tree didn't have it so we're going to the next one," said Sammy McClymonds (@sammy.mcclymonds).

At last, she had found it: pure tinted rose oil lip balm.

"Okay you guys I got it, I'm so excited," she said.

Unable to wait a second longer, she tore open the packaging while sitting in her car.

Sammy seemed a bit weary of the ingredients but for the price, she was willing to take her chances.

She was hoping to have the same dramatic results as the social media creator who first tried the find.

"Also the color on her was insane so I'm eager to see what color I get on myself," she said.

To get the full pout picture, she first removed her lip liner.

"It goes from clear to your own unique color," she explained.

Sammy showed off the clear-looking balm that smelled of roses before applying the find all over her lips.

"Oh my gosh, it's already changing."

Sammy sat back and admired her own personal shade, even giving some attitude in the rearview mirror.

"That was quick," she said in surprise.

She dabbed some product on her hand - which appeared to be a much lighter shade - as she debated whether it came out a bit too pink on her lips for her liking.

Overall, she was still loving it.

And for just over a buck, you couldn't beat the Dollar Tree buy that delivered on its color-changing promise.

"I feel like for $1.25 that's so fun it's supposed to match your own pH level."

She also really appreciated its hydrating properties.

Many of her fans were digging the look.

"I like the pink, it goes well with your hair," said one impressed follower.

"Aw thank you!" Sammy responded.

After returning home she realized she still wanted to tone down the pink.

Sammy wiped most of it off before remembering how the girl in the video had mentioned that it was a lip stain.

"I feel like it's the perfect natural pink," she explained.

And she was especially obsessed with the lip stain's long-lasting properties.

"This is so perfect for going to dinner, for making out. Now my lips are just going to look like this for the rest of the day. It's so worth $1.25 and it's the perfect color for my lips."

For those who have trouble snagging the balm at their local Dollar Tree, the buy sells for around $6 to $10 on various online shopping sites.

And there were plenty of satisfied lip-stained customers.

"I love this lip balm. The color is immediate and long-lasting and looks great," said this pleased reviewer.

This shopper had found a stockpile of the stain.

"$1.25 at Dollar Tree - I have five tubes," she said.

"It’s so pretty on you!" another remarked.
