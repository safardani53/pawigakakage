NELLA Rose's time in the I'm A Celebrity jungle has been far from a smooth one.

The Influencer has now caused concern amongst viewers that she has left the camp for good.

What happened to Nella Rose on I'm a Celeb?

On November 28, 2023, Nella Rose appeared to have "quit" the jungle after she missing from the live part of I'm A Celebrity.

She sparked concern she had walked, after Ant and Dec revealed she was away from camp.

However, they went on to explain the reason for her absence was because she was "being seen by medics".

This prompted fans to immediately speculate that she had quit.

Viewers were convinced the outspoken YouTuber and social media star had thrown in the towel after struggling through a string of high-octane challenges and clashes in camp.

Nella had previously said she had considered walking, but didn't "want to be the first to go".

Has Nella Rose left I'm a Celeb?

Nella has NOT left the I'm A Celebrity jungle, and is now back in camp.

The Sun exclusively revealed how she has rejoined the I’m A Celebrity camp after ITV bosses scrambled to rush her to medics during the live show.

An ITV spokesman said: "During last night's live broadcast, Nella Rose required medical treatment on site and was deemed unable to take part in the trial.

"Nella has since rejoined camp."

ITV insiders told The Sun that Nella's emergency was NOT linked to a trial.

She was treated immediately and was able to return to the main site within hours.

A source said: “It was unfortunate timing that Nella had to be taken out of camp just before the live scenes.

“She had to be seen by the team with a physical, medical emergency but it was dealt with swiftly.

“Nella has had a rough time so far but she is a tough cookie and will be back smiling and laughing with the other campmates in no time - this wasn’t anything to do with her mental health.”

Nella's time in camp has also included tense moments, bust-ups and clashes after she came up against Fred Sirieix in the now infamous 'dad-gate' row, plus grilling Nigel Farage on immigration.

A source said: "Nella had a real journey in camp but ultimately the strain of jungle life proved too much.

"She really struggled mentally with the trials but also kept finding herself in tension with campmates."

Who has left I'm a Celeb?

On November 27, 2023, it was confirmed that food critic Grace Dent had left I'm a Celebrity on "medical grounds".

She lasted eight days in the jungle, and the MasterChef star is expected to get her full fee.

In an emotional letter to her fellow campmates, Grace said: "My Dear campmates, I’m so sorry to let you down. I have left the camp for medical reasons.

"My heart is broken, I have loved and enjoyed getting to know you all. You’ve held me up and it’s been a pleasure being your friend through this experience.

"Leaving you all at this stage will be one of the saddest things in my life. I love you all. Your friend, Miss Grace Dent.”
