Who is Geraldine Page:

Geraldine Page is a famous Stage Actress. She was born on November 22, 1924 and her birthplace is Kirksville, MO. Geraldine is also well known as, American actress who won an Academy Award in 1985 for her role in the film The Trip to Bountiful. Her numerous Broadway and Off-Broadway credits include Alma in Summer and Smoke, Lizzie in The Rainmaker, and Olga in Three Sisters.

Geraldine is originated from United States. She was married to actor Rip Torn from 1963 until her death in 1987. She and Torn had three children: a daughter and twin sons.
