Lucky Montana Biography

Lucky Montana was born in Mamelodi, Pretoria, South Africa, between 1963 and 1968. Tshepo Lucky Montana is his full name. His astrological sign, according to his given name, is Aries.

Lucky completed his primary education at a local Mamelodi school. He enrolled at the University of Cape Town to pursue a Bachelor of Arts in Social Science. He also finished his honors degree in Social Research Methods.

Tshepo has not revealed the names or occupations of his father and mother, but we learned from sources that his father is a businessman and his mother is a housewife. They taught him the values and etiquette. According to our sources, he is of the Christian faith and has a mixed ethnic background.

Career

In 2009, Lucky was appointed CEO of the Passenger Rail Agency of South Africa (PRASA). His job was to oversee the testing and commissioning of South Africa’s new commuter rail system. He was completely dedicated to his work. He was fired from his job in 2015 for abusing his authority.

Lucky is also South Africa’s Deputy Director-General in Charge of Public Transport. His role was to inspect the work of contractors and award contracts to the most efficient ones, but he only awarded works and tenders to people he knew. He is partial, and as a result, he has stolen a large sum of money from the funds.
