PAW Patrol Live! tickets on sale NOW! The Great Pirate Adventure tour prices, dates and venues

PAW Patrol is on a roll… and coming back to an arena near you this summer.

Based on the hot TV series, PAW Patrol Live! is returning to stages with all your (children's) favourite pups for The Great Pirate Adventure.

Where can I get PAW Patrol Live! tickets? How much are they?

Tickets are on sale NOW from Ticketmaster and other leading ticket agencies.

Prices start from around £17 plus booking fee.

All children above 12 months require a ticket. Younger babies are free on the knee.

What are the dates for PAW Patrol Live: The Great Pirate Adventure?

The Great Pirate Adventure is heading to all corners of the UK.

Some venues are hosting THREE performances per day (typically at 10:00, 13:30 and 16:30) - see Ticketmaster for full details.

What’s The Great Pirate Adventure all about?

Mayor Goodway is getting everything shipshape for a big Pirate Day celebration in Adventure Bay.

However, when Cap’n Turbot falls into a dark and mysterious cavern, it’s PAW Patrol to the rescue!

The show features all your favourite pups - Chase, Marshall, Rubble, Skye, Rocky and Zuma - plus new pup Tracker, who's "all ears'. 

Land and sea, lost treasure maps, teamwork and epic adventure are all par for the course as PAW Patrol Live! The Great Adventure comes to a venue near you.
