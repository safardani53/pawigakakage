Will Arnett Is Dating Interior Designer Elizabeth Law

He’s got a new sweetheart! Will Arnett is dating interior designer Elizabeth Law, a source reveals to Us Weekly. 

The Canadian-born actor, 46, and Law, 30, were spotted leaving the Nice Guy in West Hollywood Thursday, August 25. “They’re having a lot of fun,” an insider tells Us.

“Elizabeth, who is also known as Law Slagsvol, is related to Jimmy Buffett and used to date Don Johnson’s son Jesse,” a source tells Us of The Lego Movie voice actor’s new love interest. Her interior design work can be seen on her website, Elizabeth Law Design. 

Arnett was previously linked to celebrity chef Katie Lee and actress Arielle Vandenberg following his split from Amy Poehler in 2012 after nine years of marriage. 

Poehler and Arnett’s divorce was finalized in August. According to the Associated Press, the court documents stated that neither actor would receive spousal support and that they would share joint custody of their sons, Archibald, 7, and Abel, 6. Poehler ended her two-year relationship with fellow comedian Nick Kroll in September 2015.

"Getting a divorce really sucks," Poehler wrote in her 2014 memoir, Yes Please. "Imagine spreading everything you care about on a blanket and then tossing the whole thing up in the air. The process of divorce is about loading that blanket, throwing it up, watching it all spin and worrying what stuff will break when it lands."

As for how Arnett dealt with the split? "If going to the gym obsessively for eight, nine months was my way of dealing, then let that be my worst problem," he told Details magazine in June 2013. "Life is challenging, and I'd say that there's no guidebook."

He added: “I've found my happiness — which is my kids."

Arnett voices the main character in BoJack Horseman, streaming now on Netflix.
