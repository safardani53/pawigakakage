Who is Eugene Lee Yang's partner Matthew McLean?

YOUTUBE creator, Eugene Lee Yang, is known for being one of four members of The Try Guys.

When Yang's not behind the camera filming popular content, he is spending time with his partner, Matthew McLean.

Who is Eugene Lee Yang's partner Matthew McLean?

Eugene Lee Yang and Matthew McLean went public with their relationship in 2019, although the couple had already been dating for years.

The pair moved in together in 2013 and share three dogs together - Emma, Pesto, Kimbop - McLean revealed in a 2020 interview with TryPods.

According to the 36-year-old, he met Yang at a gym.

"It's funny because we had seen each other at the gym for like a year," he told TryPods. "I talked to him first and he just asked me out right away."

Who is Eugene Lee Yang?

Yang is a South Korean-American YouTube celebrity known for being a co-founder of The Try Guys - a popular vlog-style YouTube channel created in 2014.
