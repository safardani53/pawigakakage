Cameron Crowe and Nancy Wilson to Divorce

In sad news this morning, acclaimed director Cameron Crowe and his rocker wife, Nancy Wilson, are officially pulling the plug on their 22-year marriage.

Wilson, a singer and songwriter from the rock band Heart, filed divorce papers Thursday in L.A. County Superior Court, citing irreconcilable differences.

She lists June 15, 2008, as the date of their separation.

Cameron and Nancy in a recent photo.

Crowe, who directed Jerry Maguire and Almost Famous, married Wilson on July 23, 1986. The couple have 10-year-old twin boys, William and Curtis.

Wilson is seeking joint physical and legal custody of the children and has also asked the court for spousal support. We wish them both the best …
