Jersey Shore's Deena Cortese upgrades to massive $1.4M NJ mansion boasting full bar, billiard room a

JERSEY Shore star Deena Cortese has upgraded into a massive $1.4million New Jersey home after leaving her more modest $630,000 crib.

The U.S. Sun can exclusively confirm Deena, 36, moved into the home with her husband Chris Buckner, 34, and her two boys back in August 2022.

The five-bedroom, five-bathroom mansion features a home theatre, billiards room and even a sauna room.

Deena's backyard includes paver patios, a fire pit, a pool house, and a luxury heated pool with LED lights.

The Jersey Shore star's impressive new home also comes with a custom oak bar complete with beer and wine fridges, an ice maker, a sink, a microwave, and two televisions.

Her living room and kitchen features granite countertops, wood floors, and custom chandeliers.
