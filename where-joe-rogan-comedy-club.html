Where is Joe Rogan's comedy club?

FAMED UFC color commentator and podcaster Joe Rogan has opened a new comedy club in Texas.

Now, fans want to know where it's located so they can pay it a visit.

Where is Joe Rogan's comedy club?

Joe Rogan has quite an extensive resume but his new comedy club might be one of his greatest projects to date.

The anti-cancel culture club, named Comedy Mothership, is described as an "ideal venue for comics" where they can share their dark humor without fear of retaliation.

"You can’t fire me from my own club, b****," Rogan proclaimed, via The Hollywood Reporter.

The club is located in Austin, Texas, on the city's historic Sixth Street.

While it might seem like a regular comedy club to most, Rogan has put several security measures into place to ensure the safety and protection of the comedians.

Some of those security measures, according to THR, include face scans and a ban on cell phones.

Rogan also explained that he hired “the best people” from the Comedy Store in Los Angeles to help run his club.

What has Joe Rogan said about his comedy club?

Rogan has done several things throughout the course of his career but owning a comedy club was not on his radar until he moved from Los Angeles to Austin in 2020.

During a 2021 appearance on Theo Von’s podcast, he explained that the club was a passion project.

"I felt compelled to do it," Rogan explained, via THR.

"I never wanted to own a comedy club, and I always felt like you just had to be nice to comedy club owners because you never want to be one of those people.

"But then when I knew I was moving here, and [Austin’s Capital City Comedy Club] was already closed. I was like, ‘Maybe I should buy a f***ing club, and start a club.’ And that became my focus.”

Opening night of Rogan's comedy club saw performances by David Lucas, Ron White, Tim Dillon, Roseanne Barr, and Tony Hinchcliffe.

What is Joe Rogan's net worth?

Due to Rogan's successful career, he has been able to amass a fortune.

As of March 2023, he has an estimated net worth of $120million, with an annual salary of $60million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

This estimate comes from his career as a UFC commentator and podcast host.

Since 2009, he has hosted The Joe Rogan Experience podcast, where he often discusses current events, comedy, politics, philosophy, science, and hobbies with several guests.

In 2020, he signed a podcast deal with Spotify worth roughly $100million, but the true value could be as high as $200million, according to The New York Times.

When he's not working, he can often be found spending time with his wife, Jessica Ditzel, and his three daughters.
