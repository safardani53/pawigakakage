Who is Sex Actually host Alice Levine?

ALICE Levine is one of the most sought-after faces in broadcasting today. 

Since co-creating the podcast My Dad Wrote A Porno, she has made a BIG name for herself.

Who is Alice Levine?

Alice Levine is a radio and television and radio presenter born on July 8, 1986.

Her first TV job was hosting Celebrity Bites for MTV, which later led to other shows including coverage of the EMAs.

She has hosted Big Brother's Bits On The Side with Jamie East. and was even one of the voices on the show that chatted to housemates in the diary room chair.

In January 2013, she joined BBC Radio One to present a show with Phil Taggart.

What else is Alice Levine known for?

Alice is perhaps most famous for being one of the co-creators of the popular podcast My Dad Wrote A Porno.

She met her future podcast collaborators, Jamie Morton and James Cooper while she was studying at the University of Leeds.

From 2017 to 2019, she presented backstage at the Brit Awards for ITV2 and has appeared as a panellist on 8 Out Of 10 Cats, Room 101, QI, Would I Lie to You? and Taskmaster.

After the success of her documentary Sleeping With The Far Right, she now fronts C4’s Sex Actually.

Alice has also appeared as an actress in the TV series Neighbours.

Is Alice Levine in a relationship?

Alice was previously dating rock musician Edward Ibbotson, who is known for being in the indie rock band Life in Film.

Before starting his career in a rock band, Edward was a professional cook.

He used to be the main chef at The Convenience, a restaurant with a summer roof terrace, located on Chatsworth Road in London.

Alice met Edward when he and his friends were looking for a flatmate.

In a 2014 interview with the Telegraph, Alice revealed that she and Edward moved into their own flat.

However, according to multiple reports, it is now believed that Alice is currently single.

What is Sex Actually about?

Sex Actually With Alice Levine is a TV show that explores what sex means in modern Britain.

The series was executive produced by Louis Theroux.

Presenter Alice begins by meeting three couples who “stream-cam” their sex lives for money.
