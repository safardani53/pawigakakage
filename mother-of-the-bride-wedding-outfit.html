What to wear as mother of the bride to your next wedding

MOTHER of the Bride is an important role, which means a stylish outfit.

While some mother of the brides opt for chic dresses, some prefer a co-ord complete with a jacket and matching trouser set.

While some mother's of the bride will stick with tradition and wear a hat or fascinator, some may prefer to let their outfit do the talking - and we don't blame you.

We have sifted high and low for chic, yet flattering, outfits for the mother of the bride to wear on the day of the ceremony - just don't wear white!
