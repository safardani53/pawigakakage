Who is the woman on Drake's Her Loss album cover?

DRAKE fans have been left with more questions than answers after the rapper released the cover art for new album Her Loss.

The 19-track LP album, in collaboration with 21 Savage, was released in November 2022, and featured a close shot of a young woman with jewelry in her mouth and colorful eyelashes.

Who is on the front cover of Drake's Her Loss album?  

Drake's Her Loss album cover features model Qui Yasuka, who goes by the name Suki Baby on social media.

Yasuka is a nail stylist and professional adult dancer who is based in Houston.

Born in North Carolina, the model spent much of her early years in Japan before moving to the US full time.

In a series of tweets in August 2022, she revealed she was once a budding 3D design artist and started sketching when she was in high school.

During the same period she also started doing nails and now runs her own business.

In an interview with gtalks!, Suki suggested she had always had a deep interest in grills and teeth jewelry since she was a teen, explaining that she even studied teeth anatomy to better understand how the human mouth works.  

At the time of writing, however, she is better known for her modelling and OnlyFans work as well as her content on YouTube.

The model has an active following on social media and, as of November 2022, boasts 91,000 followers on Twitter and around 30,000 on Instagram.

How did she end up on the album cover?  

Reports suggest that the cover photo of Suki is about three years old and was taken by photographer Paris Aden.

According to Complex, he claims the OVO camp reached out to model randomly requesting permission to use the shot for the album cover.

Aden told the outlet: "Drake just saw the photo I guess, and they asked if they could use it for the album.

“I shot that s**t a long time ago. It was her profile pic and he probably fell in love with what he seen.”

Although it remains unclear why Drake chose the image, many have speculated that Suki’s ties with Houston may have been a factor in his decision.

Rapper Lil Yachty later suggested on Instagram that he was the one who found the image and chose it for the cover.

He wrote in a post: “I Chose This Cover Because This Photo Is So Raw… So Authentic.. Not Fabricated.. Suki Can And Will Only Be Suki.

“MY BROTHRRR @aristatalovich BROUGHT IT TO LIFE…. HER LOSS. F**K WHO AINT WIT US.”

When was the Her Loss album released?  

Drake and Savage 21 officially released their album Her Loss on November, 4, 2022.

The 19-track LP includes Drake and 21 Savage’s song Jimmy Cooks as well as the final track from Drake’s summer LP, Honestly, Nevermind.

The album only has one feature, with Travis Scott guesting on Pussy & Millions.

Where can I listen to Her Loss?

You can stream Drake’s Her Loss on Spotify and Apple Music.
