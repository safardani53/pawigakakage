Who is Tammy Faye Bakker:

Tammy Faye Bakker is a famous Religious Leader. She was born on March 7, 1942 and her birthplace is International Falls, MN. Tammy is also well known as, Christian singer and televangelist who co-hosted the popular PTL Club show with her husband from 1976 until 1987. She was forced to leave PTL after revelations that $287,000 had been paid from the organization to buy the silence of a woman, who claimed her husband, Jim Bakker, had raped her.

Tammy is originated from United States. Her former husband Jim Bakker went to prison over accounting fraud.
