How much does Arsene Wenger's coat cost?

Arsene Wenger's Arsenal coat has a standard retail price of £110 at the Arsenal club shop.

But it was recently on sale for just £60.

Unfortunately, all Wenger coats at the club shop are currently sold out.

The club have yet to announce whether more will go on sale before the end of the season.

Wenger's current coat is exclusive to the Arsenal shop although certain online outlets are selling older replicas, but all appear to have sold out at the moment.
