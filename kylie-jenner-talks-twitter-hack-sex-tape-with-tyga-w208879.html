Kylie Jenner Talks Twitter Hack, Sex Tape With Tyga

Setting two records straight. Kylie Jenner took to Snapchat on Sunday, June 5, to tell fans her Twitter account had been hacked, so the array of rude, lewd and unpleasant tweets (involving stars such as Justin Bieber) streaming out of her account were not from her.

And while she was at it, the 18-year-old reality star decided it was a good idea to put an end to another untruth making the rounds at the moment: that images from a leaked sex tape of her and ex-boyfriend Tyga might be showing up online.

“Mmmm, so my Twitter was hacked. I don’t really care,” she said in her Snapchat as she chews on a pretzel. “I’m just letting them have fun.”

But since one of those moments of fun (alongside a declaration that “i love being so famous with no talent”) related to the existence of a sex tape, Jenner thought she’d just shut that rumor down quick.

“Well my sex tape with tyga was trash,” the fake Kylie Jenner tweeted.

“Everyone is like ‘leak the sex tape,'” Jenner said in her second video. “Guys, you are never going to see a sex tape from me. It’s not going to happen.”

Jenner is the latest celebrity to face the awkwardness of a Twitter hack. Last week Katy Perry was a victim, with her hacker stirring up old beef between herself and Taylor Swift.

Jenner’s hacker played along with the same joke during her string of false messages. “Miss u both @taylorswift13 @katyperry,” the mystery hacker tweeted.

But while some of the hacker’s posts were harmless celeb-land gags, others totally crossed the line, with racist and lewd outbursts. The messages have now been deleted.
