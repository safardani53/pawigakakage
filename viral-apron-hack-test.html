I tried the viral apron trick to make my body look better - people are desperate to visit the coffee

UNIFORMS can be hard to style in a way that flatters your body shape.

One coffee shop worker tried the viral apron trick to make her work look more form-fitting, and people thought it worked great.

Katie Nichols spends her weekdays behind the counter serving at Costa Coffee.

And that means she's usually wearing a boxy apron.

Because her uniform wasn't to her liking, Katie tried the viral apron trick to shape her body better in a recent video.

She starts by folding the denim apron in half where the outside of the top isn't showing.

Next, Katie ties the apron around her back.

Again, the top half is folded down.

She crosses the ties and brings them around her front.

Katie secures the apron with a bow in the middle of her stomach.
