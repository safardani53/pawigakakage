Man stabbed outside TK Maxx as cops seal off Leeds shopping centre

POLICE today swooped on a shopping centre after a man was stabbed outside a TK Maxx.

Emergency crews were scrambled to the Crown Point Retail Park in Leeds early this morning after the bloodbath unfolded.

The victim was rushed to hospital but his injuries are not thought to be life-threatening.

Police have now cordoned off the busy shopping area while they investigate.

Shoppers are able to enter the store but police are stood outside.

It is not clear if there have been any arrests.

A statement from West Yorkshire Police read: "At 7.17am today, police were called to Crown Point Retail Park where it was reported a man had suffered stab wounds.

"The man was taken to hospital where his injuries were found not to be life threatening.

"Enquiries are ongoing by Leeds District CID into how the man came to be injured."
