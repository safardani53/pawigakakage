Jordan Emanuel vying after Kory Keefer gives Winter House fans second-hand embarrassment

After two successful seasons, Bravo’s Winter House is back this year. Of course, it isn’t all ski lessons and singing around the fire, this lot are conjuring up cocktails based on their exes and hooking up from day one. Fans may have expected drama from season 3, but a love triangle from the offset? Some are simply confused and others have “second-hand embarrassment.”

Jordan and Kory on Winter House

Summer House: Martha’s Vineyard star Jordan Emanuel is ready for some action on the slopes this year – and we don’t just mean skiing.

After meeting co-star Kory Keefer on Winter House, Jordan expresses an interest in him. However, another cast member does, too.

Below Deck Mediterranean’s Malia White is also getting in her feels over Kory.

The Bravo stars form a love triangle of sorts, despite Kory being in some kind of ‘situationship’ back home.

View Tweet

Jordan has fans ‘cringing’

Reality TV stars are no strangers to forming connections on their various shows and judging by the Winter House crew’s first few episodes together, they’re almost all open to love – in some capacity.

During season 3 episode 3, Jordan shoots her shot on her crush and ends up in tears.

Both Jordan and Malia were interested in Kory. But, things head south when Jordan thinks she sees Malia and Kory ‘kiss’ in the kitchen. But, the Below Deck star explains she and Kory didn’t kiss – she was simply spitting a drink into his mouth – what’s weird about that?

Despite her heartache, Jordan’s tearful scenes had lots of fans “cringing.”

Some took to X (Twitter) to share their thoughts: “Everyone going after Kory is a complete joke. It was literally communicated to all of them that he has someone. They all look dumb af, especially Jordan crying over him like what? I can’t wait for Sam to show up lol.”

More tweeted their thoughts about it being “obvious” that Kory was in a ‘situationship’, and more wrote: “I’m getting secondhand embarrassment from Jordan.”

Kory Keefer was a ‘dumb boy’

Speaking to TVLine at BravoCon 2023, Kory confirms that he and his girlfriend, Sam Feher, are “great.”

He added that the show was filmed “like, eight months ago,” and that it was a “weird time,” in their relationship where they “didn’t know what was going on.”

Kory added that he was a “dumb boy,” but now he’s a “good boy” and asks fans not to “hate” him.
