Natalie Jane Imbruglia Biography

In the early 2000s, Natalie was spotted with David Schwimmer. David Schwimmer is an actor from Untied States known for starring the role of Ross Geller in the TV series Friends. He is also a director and producer. This relation was short lived.

After that Natalie began her romantic love affairs with Daniel Johns. She began dating him as her boyfriend. Daniel Johns is a great musician and singer from Australia. He is also popular for being the former front man of the rock band Silverchair. After being in love for several years, they announced their engagement in January 2003 and they finally got married on 31st of December 2003. She was happy to be his loving spouse but they got divorced in early 2008.

In her mid teens, Natalie moved to Sydney with her family. There she took ballet, tap and highland dancing lessons as she was also interested to pursue her career as a dancer. At the age of sixteen, she also appeared in several TV ads and commercials. To focus on her acting career, she dropped out from her high school. In 1991, she won the role of Beth Brennan in the popular Australian soap Neighbours. She appeared in this soap opera till 1994. In 1997, she starred in the TV series Law of the Land. In the late 1990s, she also appeared in the Saturday Night Live as its guest. She made her feature film debut in 2003 by starring the role of Lorna Campbell in the movie Johnny English. She has also been pursuing her musical career since the mid 1990s. In 1994, she moved to London and she signed a recording deal with BMG. She began recording her debut single Torn and it was finally released in 1997. Her debut album Left of the Middle was also released on 24th of November 1997. The actual figure about her salary is unknown but her net worth is $14 million.
