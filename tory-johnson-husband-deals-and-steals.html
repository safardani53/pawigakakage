Who is Tory Johnsons husband?

TORY Johnson is known for her Deals & Steals segment on ABC's Good America but the shopping guru is more than just about a bargain.

Johnson is a married mother and wife to a fellow journalist.

Who is Tory Johnson's husband?

Tory is married to Peter Johnson for more than twenty years.

On her Instagram page, she shares photos of Peter and their twins, Emma, an ecommerce expert, and Jake, a veterinary school student.

Peter was a long-time journalist at USA Today before taking a buyout in 2007. He was a media beat reporter who wrote the Inside TV column, followed by Media Mix.

When Peter stepped down from USA Today, TV Newser said he was, "respected and adored and occasionally feared by TV execs for his sharp wit and "knowledge of where every single body is buried."

How did Tory Johnson get her start?

Tory Johnson grew up in Miami and became the first female to become Florida state debate champion in high school.

She attended Emerson College but dropped out for a chance to work at ABC News. She then jumped to NBC News but after two years was unexpectedly fired.

Those events prompted her to start Women for Hire. It hosts high career expos attracting talent and leading employers.

She's a #1 New York Times bestselling author of The Shift which documents how she lost 62 pounds a year after fearing she'd lose her job.

What is Deals & Steals?

Tory is the ecommerce editor at ABC News who created the series.

Each week, she finds great buys on popular items chosen by her. She passes along the discount to viewers.

Shoppers have spent hundreds of millions of dollars on Tory's picks.

Tory also curates exclusive deals for ABC's The View and LIVE with Kelly & Ryan.
