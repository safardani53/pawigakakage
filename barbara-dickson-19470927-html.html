Barbara Dickson (Pop Singer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Barbara Ruth Dickson is a Scottish singer with hits including "I Know Him So Well", "Answer Me" and "January January". Dickson has placed fifteen albums on the UK Album Chart from 1977 to the present, and has had a number of successful singles, four of which reached the Top 20 on the UK Singles Chart. The Scotsman newspaper describes her as Scotland's best-selling singer in terms of single and album success in the UK since 1976. She is also a two-time Olivier Award-winning actress, with roles like Viv Nicholson in the musical Spend Spend Spend, and is Mrs. Johnstone in Willy Russell's Blood Brothers musical. On television, she starred as Anita Braithwaite in Band of Gold.
