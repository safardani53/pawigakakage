Morgan Heritage (Band) - Members profile, Facts, Wiki, Achievements, Net worth 2024 (updated)

Morgan Heritage is a Grammy-nominated Jamaican reggae band formed in 1994 by five children of reggae artist Denroy Morgan, namely Peter "Peetah" Morgan, Una Morgan, Roy "Gramps" Morgan, Nakhamyah "Lukes" Morgan, and Memmalatel "Mr. Mojo" Morgan. They have toured internationally and released a number of reggae albums.

OriginBrooklyn, New York, Springfield, Massachusetts, Jamaica

Five piece group formed by the five children of Jamaican reggae artist Denroy Morgan. Their 2015 album Strictly Roots won a 2016 Grammy Award for Best Reggae Album.

Between 2004 and 2005, the group put out compilation albums called Morgan Heritage Family and Friends, Vol. 1, Morgan Heritage Family and Friends, Vol. 2 and Morgan Heritage Family and Friends, Vol. 3 (2005). They also released a number of live albums.
