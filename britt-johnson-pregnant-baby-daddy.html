Britt Johnson Is Pregnant But Is Yet To Reveal Baby Daddy

Britt Johnson, the renowned sports reporter, announced the exciting news that she is pregnant on June 24, 2023, via an Instagram post. 

The Instagram post featured a heartwarming video, capturing the joy as she lovingly posed for the camera with her blossoming belly while enjoying some fun moments at the beach. 

Alongside the touching video, the soon-to-be mother expressed, “Growing you has been my favorite part.”

Similarly, on July 4, 2023, she shared another clip that showed several pictures of her proudly flaunting her growing baby bump, writing, “You never know life until it grows inside you.”

As the pregnancy progressed, Johnson continued to share glimpses of her journey with her followers. On July 15, 2023, she let everyone in on a personal decision: she had chosen to wait until the birth of the baby to discover its gender. 

In a video, she playfully predicted the baby’s gender based on various “old wives tales.” 

Additionally, on August 3, 2023, she posted several pictures of herself in a vibrant orange dress that beautifully accentuated her baby bump with the caption, “It’s a Big Momma thang.”

Britt Johnson flaunting her growing belly bump. (Source: iambrittjohnson/Instagram)

However, Johnson seemed to omit the identity of the baby’s father purposefully. She never once mentioned anything about the baby daddy, not even a subtle hint or the name of the father.

Her pregnancy announcement took the internet by storm as fans and followers expressed their excitement and well-wishes for the soon-to-be mother. 

While Johnson’s fans were showering her with support and affection, they couldn’t help but wonder about the baby daddy’s identity. 

“Which NBA player did she finally get to impregnant her,” a fan humorously commented, while most of the comments read, “Whos the baby daddy though?”

Britt Johnson remains hush about her boyfriend

It seems like Johnson might be dating someone as she shared a video of her with a mysterious guy with whom she went on vacation in Mexico.

The video was uploaded on July 20, 2022, on her Instagram account, where she showed the different places she visited with “baecation” written in her video, which has since been removed.

The caption of the video said, “Actually, the best gift you could give her is a lifetime of adventures.” 

The video included some sweet pictures of Johnson and her possible boyfriend, where Johnson can be seen being all lovey-dovey with the guy enjoying every moment with him.

The video also included what seemed like a surprise from the guy when he decorated the room and the tub filled with rose petals, and Johnson seemed quite surprised and happy. 

However, the name of the mysterious guy wasn’t revealed at the time, and now the identity of her baby daddy is also yet to be disclosed.

Until Johnson doesn’t confirm that the baby daddy and the guy she went on vacation with are the same guy, it is unsure whether they are indeed the same.

Who is Britt Johnson?

Johnson, born in California, grew up in a military family, which allowed her to explore different parts of the country, as per her official website. 

From an early age, she developed a strong sense of competition and leadership through her involvement in cheerleading. 

Her love for sports blossomed when she witnessed the awe-inspiring performances of the Dallas Cowboys, particularly the players like Emmitt Smith, Michael Irvin, and Deion Sanders.

Enrolling at California State University, Fresno, Johnson pursued a major in Communication and a minor in Special Event Planning. 

During her college years, she interned at KSEE 24 News Station, an NBC affiliate, where she learned various aspects of the media industry, from production to editing. 

However, it was her opportunity to report from the sidelines at Fresno State’s sporting events that brought her “fun.” 

She later joined the Los Angeles Clippers organization, coordinating private events and managing customer relations for high-profile clients. 

Despite her successes, Johnson felt a yearning for something more, which prompted her to take classes to enhance her natural broadcasting abilities. Afterward, she launched her website and weekly viral sports shows on her personal YouTube channel. 

The anchor worked as a Weekend Sports Anchor in Louisiana, a Sports Reporter in Torrance, CA, and has interviewed numerous favorite athletes and celebrities at red carpet events and sporting occasions. 

She also appeared on nationally broadcast radio and TV networks like Fox Sports and MSNBC and has received several awards.

Notably, Johnson spent over five years as the primary host for The Fumble, where she played a crucial role in growing its audience to over 1.5 million subscribers. 

Most recently, she served as the team reporter, producer, and content creator for the iconic Dallas Cowboys and currently serves as the lead reporter for the NBA on TNT.
