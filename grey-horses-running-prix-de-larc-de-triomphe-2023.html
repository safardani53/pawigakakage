What grey horses are running at the Prix de l'Arc de Triomphe 2023?

ALL eyes will be heavily focussed on the Prix de l'Arc de Triomphe at the iconic race gets underway.

Frankie Dettori is in action for the final time at Longchamp, as he rides Free Wind.

Dettori will look to better his third place finish with Torquator Tasso from last year.

You may be on the lookout for grey horses during the huge event, as we also look at the last time one of its kind won the big prize.

What grey horses are running in the Prix de l'Arc de Triomphe?

There are NO grey horses running this year at the Prix de l'Arc de Triomphe.

When did a grey horse last win the Prix de l'Arc de Triomphe?

Not too long ago - last year in fact!

Alpinista, a grey mare, stormed home to victory for British trainer Sir Mark Prescott.

The six-year-old beat out competition from Frankie Dettori's Torquator Tasso.

"She's does everything right, she made training easy, she's got a little bit better every time," Prescott said of Alpinista.
