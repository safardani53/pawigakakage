'Sister Wives' Family Guide: Who Are Kody Brown's Spouses, Kids?

Sister Wives star Kody Brown has his hands full with wife Robyn Brown and his three former partners, Meri Brown, Janelle Brown and Christine Brown.

Kody’s family — which includes 18 total children— was thrust into the spotlight in September 2010 when Sister Wives premiered. At the time, Kody was courting his fourth wife, Robyn, and still legally married to first wife Meri.

Kody divorced Meri in 2014 to legally wed Robyn and adopt her three kids from a past relationship. While Kody remained in spiritual unions with Meri, Janelle and Christine, their relationships soon began to fizzle out.

“I feel like my wives are living four separate lives, but I don’t feel like I’m living four separate lives,” Kody exclusively told Us Weekly in February 2021. “I’m sort of strung out on this relationship conveyor belt. … In fact, it seems like my wives are in four different worlds.”
