Who is MyKayla Skinner:

MyKayla Skinner is a famous Gymnast. She was born on December 9, 1996 and her birthplace is Mesa, AZ. MyKayla is also well known as, Artistic gymnast who had a breakout year in 2014, winning the World Artistic Gymnastics Championships. She also won gold in four categories at the Pan American Championships that same year.

MyKayla is originated from United States. She was teammates with Simone Biles on Team U.S.A. at the 2014 World Artistic Gymnastics Championships.
