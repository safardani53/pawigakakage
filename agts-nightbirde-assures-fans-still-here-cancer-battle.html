AGT's Nightbirde shares new photo &amp; assures fans she's 'still here' after admitting she weighs 85 lb

NIGHTBIRDE shared an update with fans after confessing that she only weighs 85 pounds in her brutal cancer battle.

The America's Got Talent star was given a two percent chance of survival after learning that her cancer had spread to her lungs, spine, and liver.

Nightbirde, formally known as Jane Marczewski, took to Instagram on Friday to share a message of hope with her fans.

The singer posted a photo while enjoying the sun on her patio in a denim romper, peach beanie, and heart-shaped glasses.

The TV personality captioned her shot: "Still here," as fans raced to the comments to offer words of support.

"You’re amazing. Keep shining," one gushed, while a second wrote: "You are the energetic light we so desperately need. Simply beautiful inside and out."

AGT judge Howie Mandel also shared his words of encouragement, penning: "We’re here with you."

FRIGHTENING NEWS

Nightbirde's post followed the shocking news that she had dropped to 85 pounds amid her battle with cancer.

In a recent photo, the vocalist smiled for the camera while holding on to an IV pole, and captured the shot: "Ever seen 85 pounds look so good????"

UNFORTUNATE ENDING

The 30-year-old talent, who impressed judge Simon Cowell so much with her audition that he decided to send the singer straight to the live shows, announced on-air that her cancer had taken a turn for the worse.

In July 2020 Nightbirde went into remission, although her cancer later returned.

The musician has been open about her struggles, and recently thanked her fans for their support.

"Hi, I just want to say thank you so much for all the encouragement, support and love, from all over the world," she wrote on Instagram.

"Thanks for believing in me and cheering me on and believing for impossible things."

She also told her followers she was "excited to see" how her "story plays out."

Nightbirde closed: "I'm so glad you guys are along for the ride now. So I'm excited to see what happens if we don't give up, all of us together.

"I love you guys."

WORDS OF TRUTH

Earlier this month, the TV star shared a poem she wrote detailing how she "wants to die."

It read: "If I were to die
It would be with the reflection of the mountains in my eyes
I would die with sun-pink cheeks
And a mouth full of blackberries

"I want to die while my heart is still a greenhouse for hope
All my wild dreams as seedlings in egg cartons
Reaching toward the window

"I cannot die yellow and hungry
I will not die in sterile air

"But I would like to die
While the fireflies are still glowing
Morse coding their poetry for a cynical earth

"I would like to die like Joan of Arc
With dignity and urgency and stubbornness
A watercolor portrait in the night
A sight to behold, a hero in flames."

WINNING AND LOSING

In mid-September, Nightbirde spoke about her grief over missing the AGT finale, after being forced to leave the show.

"I bet you never saw someone win so hard and lose so hard at the same time. This isn’t how the story was supposed to go."
